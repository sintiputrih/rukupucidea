Meet Travis Rudolph parents Darryl Rudolph & Linda Rudolph

Darryl Rudolph and his wife Linda Rudolph are celebrity parents who happen to be the parents of a former American football wide receiver, Travis Rudolph.

Linda Rudolph is the mother of the former football player. Apart from her name, there is no further information about her on the internet since she has decided to live a private life to avoid the spotlight.

Darryl Rudolph on the other hand is the father of Travis. He was a maintenance worker. He is no more since he passed away in April 2017, days before the 2017 NFL Draft.

He was shot and killed while working as a maintenance worker at a strip club in West Palm Beach, Florida. Police said Darryl was working in a back storage room when a coworker, in an adjacent room, accidentally discharged a rifle while moving it off a shelf. The bullet travelled through the wall and struck Darryl in the neck.

The two were married for years before parting ways in 2017. Apart from the former NFL player, they have two other children – a son and a daughter who have been identified as Darryl Rudolph, Jr. and Terrenie Gosier-Pannell respectively.
