Kim Kardashian Regrets How &quot;Fast&quot; Her Relationship With Pete Davidson Evolved

Publish date: 2024-04-16

Kim Kardashian is sharing regrets she has about her relationship with Pete Davidson.

In the newest episode of The Kardashians, the Skims mogul had a heart-to-heart with younger sisters Kendall and Kylie Jenner about what she learned from dating the comedian. She admitted to her siblings that she “jumped into another relationship so fast” after getting divorced from ex-husband Kanye West. (Kardashian and West announced their separation in January 2021.)

“It got my mind away from stuff, and that’s not a way to run from things,” Kardashian said. She repeated a new mantra: “It's better to deal. Heal … Deal, heal, and then feel.”

When it comes to dealing with all the controversy currently surrounding West, the mom of four said she doesn’t want to look at their time together “negatively.”

“I’m not going to take every experience and bad thing that happened to me and carry that into my life,” she said. “What am I supposed to be learning from this? How is this going to make me a better person?”

Cindy Ord/MG22//Getty Images

Kardashian previously voiced this sentiment in an earlier episode of The Kardashians.

“You obviously learn from every situation, and the one thing I learned from [the] last situation was the media made me feel like I was in a very serious relationship so quickly,” she said in a confessional interview. “I just want to sneak around a little bit. There’s nothing wrong with wanting to sneak!”

Kardashian and Davidson dated for nine months before ultimately breaking up in August 2022.
