Nicole Eggert of Baywatch Wants Smaller Boobs: Watch a Botched Clip!

Publish date: 2024-01-17

No more bouncing on the beach for this Baywatch babe! Nicole Eggert has left her lifeguarding days behind her, and on Tuesday, April 21’s episode of Botched, she visits doctors Terry Dubrow and Paul Nassif to have her breasts reduced.

“I’m a mom with a 3-year-old at my hip,” she says in a sneak peek. “There are certainly no roles where I’ll be ripping my shirt off. Nobody’s seeing my boobs! I would be so much happier with a couple scars and just have a better life with them.”

But the doctors aren’t happy with her plan to reduce to a B-cup. “That’s a problem because if you go too small her breast width won’t match her breast projection and she’ll just look very flat,” Dubrow says. “It’s all about proportion.”
