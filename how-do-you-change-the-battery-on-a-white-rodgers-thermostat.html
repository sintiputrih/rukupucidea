How do you change the battery on a White Rodgers thermostat?

Publish date: 2024-05-12

How to Put a Battery in a White-Rodgers Thermostat Look at the back of the thermostat if you have not used it yet. Turn the thermostat to off and remove it from the wall base. Turn over the thermostat and remove the back of the battery panel. Remove the old batteries and discard accordingly. Insert the new batteries and snap the back of the battery panel into place.
