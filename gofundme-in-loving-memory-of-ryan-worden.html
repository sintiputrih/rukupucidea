GoFundMe: In Loving Memory of Ryan Worden

Publish date: 2024-06-15

Honoring the Memory of Ryan Worden: A Fundraiser for His Family

With heavy hearts, we come together to create this fundraiser to assist the family of Ryan Worden with the financial burden of funeral and burial costs. The sudden and tragic loss of Ryan has left his family unprepared for the emotional and financial challenges they now face.

Ryan Worden made a profound impact on the lives of countless individuals. His unwavering friendship, infectious smile, and compelling stories brought joy and warmth to all who knew him. Ryan was not only a loyal friend but a dedicated family man who always made time for his loved ones.

Ryan Worden leaves behind two sons, Kyler, 17 years old, and Brayden, 9 years old. The financial strain of this tragedy has only added to the immense grief they bear. They have not only lost their father but also their provider.

While nothing can bring back their beloved Dad, we can come together as a community to alleviate some of the financial burdens that Kyler and Brayden now face. We are confident that with the thousands of lives Ryan touched, we can make a significant difference in supporting his family and his boys during this difficult time.

All donations will go directly to Ryan’s family to help them provide him with the funeral and memorial he deserves. We have set a high goal because any excess funds beyond the funeral expenses will be available to support Kyler and Brayden with their needs.

We understand that not everyone may be in a position to make a financial contribution, and that is completely understandable. If you can offer other forms of support, such as the use of a property for a memorial, donated catering, or any other assistance, please don’t hesitate to reach out.
