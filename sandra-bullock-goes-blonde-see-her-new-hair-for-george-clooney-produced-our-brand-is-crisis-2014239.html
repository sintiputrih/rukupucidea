Sandra Bullock Goes Blonde: See Her New Hair for Our Brand Is Crisis

Publish date: 2024-07-23

Blonde hair, don’t care! Sandra Bullock was spotted on the streets of New Orleans, Louis., on Monday, Sept. 22, with a brand new blonde ‘do.

The Gravity actress, 50, is a well-documented brunette who has long boasted silky, chocolate locks. But for her role as a political consultant in her latest film—the George Clooney-produced comedy, Our Brand Is Crisis—Bullock lightened up with a sandy blonde color. (No word yet on whether the honey hue is thanks to a fresh dye job or a masterful wig.)

This isn’t the first time the comedienne has gone blonde for a role: In 2009’s The Blind Side, for which Bullock won her first Oscar, the sassy star rocked a heavily highlighted ‘do, complete with feathered bangs, for the role of Leigh Anne Tuohy. 

That same year, the Miss Congeniality lead took on All About Steve opposite Bradley Cooper, which required her to model a choppy bob to complement her zany character, Mary.
