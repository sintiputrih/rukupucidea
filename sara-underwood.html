Sara Underwood Bio, Wiki, Age, Height, Husband, Daughter, Boston 25 News, Salary and Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-05-24

Sara Underwood is an American Emmy Award-winning Journalist and Television Host who was born and brought up in Palo Alto, California . Currently, she works at FOX/Boston 25 Morning News in Boston, as the Co-Host and Anchor of the 5 a.m. to 9 a.m. news cast.

Prior to joining the Boston 25 Morning News team in 2008, she worked at WBZ-TV and its sister station WSBK in Boston as an Anchor of the 6 PM newscast.

Sara worked at WTTG, the FOX-owned-and-operated station in Washington, D.C. as a Reporter and at WDTN, the ABC affiliate in Dayton, Ohio as an Anchor and Reporter, where she was awarded an Emmy Award for breaking news coverage. She began her journalism career at KIEM-TV in Eureka, California as a News Intern.
