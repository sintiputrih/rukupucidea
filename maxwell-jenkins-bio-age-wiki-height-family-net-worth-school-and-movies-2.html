Maxwell Jenkins Lost in Space

He was cast as Will Robinson, the youngest child in the Robinson Family in Netflix’s 2018 American science fiction television series Lost in Space. Will forms a special connection with the Robot. He was born eleven years before the Robinson family was lost in space, in a premature state. He needed intensive care as a baby and lost 3 ounces shortly after birth

He was happily shocked when he was bailed out by the Robot. Even though the shape of the Robots was somewhat grotesque and frightening at first, he developed a bond with Will and took on a more human-like appearance to make him feel more at home.

Will led the Robot back to his Jupiter spaceship, where he was able to save Wills’s sister Judy and the spaceship from underwater thanks to his ability to melt ice and powerful energy. As the Jupiter 2 was sent hurtling in space to a watery world, Will was separated from his Robot. He played with a toy version of the Robot often, chatting to it as if it was the Robot he had been friends with.
