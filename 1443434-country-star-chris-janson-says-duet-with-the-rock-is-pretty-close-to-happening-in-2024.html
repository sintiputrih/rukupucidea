Country Star Chris Janson Says Duet With The Rock Is Pretty Close To Happening In 2024

Publish date: 2024-05-26

Chris Janson is optimistic that he’ll get to harmonize on the mic with The Rock this year.

Janson recently spoke with Audacy’s Katie & Company about if a song with The Rock is in the pipeline. Janson and The Rock became friends after reminiscing about their Nashville roots and love of country music.

Chris Janson previously pitched a duet with The Rock on social media. Now, he says that is closer to happening, but he’s mindful of his friends’ spotlight and schedule.

“I’d say [a song] is pretty close [to happening] – when it happens, it happens, and it can be quick. It can be just like spur of the moment, ‘Okay, we’re gonna do it Tuesday.’ And so I think that’s kind of where it is right now. As I do consider him a friend, he’s also an extremely famous friend,” Janson explained, “and even though he doesn’t act that way, he is, and I respect that very much.

“And so, I like to give that space…I’m sure he’s got plenty of buddies who hammer all the time about stuff, and I’m not that guy… I want to be very mindful of his situation, and he has a lot going on – I would say probably ’24 you could probably look [for a song with him] and certainly some live collaboration moments between he and I singing…”
