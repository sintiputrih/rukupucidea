Get To Know Caleb Coffee Parents: Chassy And Jason Coffee!

Publish date: 0001-01-01

TikToker Caleb Coffee is miraculously alive, talking, and in good spirits after suffering a terrible mishap. He was taken to hospital on 16 August 2023 after falling off a cliff while hiking in Hawaii, according to a GoFundMe page initiated by his sister Peyton Coffee.

Caleb, his sister Peyton, and the whole Coffee Vining Family have been known to internet people for their sweet and fun dynamic relationship as seen on their social media pages and vlogs. In this writing, we shall especially talk about Chassy Coffee, the matriarch of the family, and Jason Coffee, the father of course.

Who Are TikTok Star Caleb Coffee Parents?

Caleb Coffee is the son of Chassy and Jason Coffee. He was born to them on 28 March 2005. So, lately, when he was airlifted to the emergency room with a spinal fracture and broken femur, elbow, and wrist, he was just 18 years old.

Amid the recent medical emergency, Caleb’s dad Jason took time to thank followers for their kind messages. Sharing on his Instagram Story that Caleb remains stable and conscious, he asked people to continue praying for it is “going to be a long road to recovery”. Nevertheless, he gushed, Caleb is strong and he is going to get through this.

Meet Chassy Coffee, Caleb Coffee Mother

Chassy Coffee is the least out there on social media as compared to the other members of Coffee. Nevertheless, she is as much popular and loved by the Coffee family fandom.

  • Chassy Coffee Age

Chassy Coffee was born on 27 November 1977. So, she reached the age of 45 in 2022.

  • Chassy Coffee Job

Chassy Coffee is a homemaker.

  • Is Chassy Coffee On Facebook?

Yes. Chassy Coffee did have a Facebook account as of 18 August 2023. However, she had not been much active here unlike her other social media pages.

On her IG @chassycoffee, with 257 posts and 18.5K followers, she constantly showed glimpses of her life as a mother and wife. Her TikTok account (@chassycoffee) though seemed temporarily deactivated at this point.

Meet Jason Coffee, Caleb Coffee Father

Jason Coffee is the most favored one among the Coffee Family one can tell. Yet, despite being so much out there on social media not much is known about his parents, siblings, or early life as he does not share details about these sides of his life.

So, the only way the world has seen Jason is as a completely devoted family man, a good husband, and a great father.

  • Jason Coffee Age

Jason Coffee was born on 12 August 1980, making him 43 years old in 2023.

  • Jason Coffee Job

On his social media profile, Jason admits to having had a career as an entertainer at TikTok since 26 August 2015, an entertainer at Vine since 27 January 2013, and as a content creator and videographer overall since 8 November 2008.

While on LinkedIn, he mentions being a social media influencer since January 2008. He goes on to tell that he has made branded content for companies such as Microsoft, Lenovo, M&Ms, DiGiorno, Pringles, and various Music Producers.

  • Is Jason Coffee On Facebook?

Yes. Jason Coffee can be found on Facebook. However, as compared to his other social media platforms, he has been less busy here.

On his IG @jasoncoffee, he enjoyed a massive 761K followers, and on TikTok @jasoncoffee there were as many as 21.6M followers. There also is a YouTube channel @jasoncoffee.

Jason and his family have been making videos online since 2013 when the Vine app first came out. Since then their online family has grown to over 50 million followers on Tik Tok, Instagram, Twitter, and Snapchat.

Related FAQs

  • Are Caleb Coffee Parents Still Married?

Indeed. Caleb Cofee’s parents were still married as of the time of this writing. The two have been in this internet’s beloved marriage since 28 April 2003.

Fast forward to today, Caleb’s dad is just as fond of Chassy, his wife. He can be seen calling her “full of light” and loves to tell everyone how she has helped him see clearly during some of his darkest days. He keeps on talking about Chassy being the love of his life and of him not being able to imagine his life without her.

  • How Many Kids Do Caleb Coffee Parents Have?

Including him, Caleb Coffee’s parents have three kids. The others are Caleb’s sister Peyton and brother Isaac.

Isaac Coffee is the eldest of them all. Born on 23 July 1999, he reached the age of 24 in 2023. The internet remembers him as the Vine star who long ago accumulated more than 150K followers by posting short dance clips to popular songs.

As for Peyton, she turned 19 on 28 January 2004. She also is famous on the internet for her comedic content as well as the occasional dance video. She earned over 20 million views for an October 2021 TikTok video in which she and Caleb were seen eating hot sauce that she felt was way too hot. Long before this, at the beginning of 2013, Peyton first appeared on the web as a guest in her father’s videos.

  • Where Do Caleb Coffee Parents Reside?

The whole Coffee family, including the mom and dad, are natives of Sacramento, California. Together, all of them moved to Hawaii in 2013. They have been based there ever since.
