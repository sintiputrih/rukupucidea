Acapulco Cast Photos Of The Apple TV+ Series Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-04-12

‘Acapulco’ is a new Apple TV+ series that premiered on October 8. The story begins in 1984 and follows twenty-something Máximo Gallardo (Enrique Arrizon), whose dream comes true when he becomes a cabana boy at the hottest resort in Acapulco, Las Colinas. However, he realizes the job is more complicated than he ever anticipated. He faces a demanding clientele, a complicated home life, and so much more. The story is told through the eyes of the older Máximo, played by Eugenio Derbez. ‘Acapulco’ features a terrific ensemble cast that also includes Fernando Carsa, Damián Alcázar, Camila Perez, Chord Overstreet, Vanessa Bauche, Regina Reynoso, Raphael Alejandro, Jessica Collins, Rafael Cebrián, Regina Orozco, and Carlos Corona.

The show was filmed on location in Mexico around Mismaloya. The cast and crew all stayed in a hotel throughout the entirety of the shoot. See more photos of the ‘Acapulco’ cast in our gallery now.
