Thom Yorke Reveals Radiohead Tour Plans

Publish date: 2024-05-19

LONDON (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Since releasing their eighth album, The King of Limbs, Radiohead fans have only been able to catch the band for a surprise set at the Glastonbury Festival.

However, with two September shows in NYC on the docket as well as performances on Saturday Night Live and the Colbert Report, Radiohead are peeking out from behind the curtain.

In co-hosting Giles Peterson's BBC Radio 1 show on Wednesday night, Radiohead frontman Thom Yorke revealed the band's plan to tour in 2012.

"The idea is to go out and play next year on and off during the year," says Yorke, who added that Portishead member Clive Deamer may join the group on tour as an auxiliary musician, according to Rolling Stone.

— Crystal Lynn Huntoon
