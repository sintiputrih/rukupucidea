JUDGE FAITH JENKINS AND BABY SKYLAR CELEBRATE KENNY LATTIMORE'S #1 BILLBOARD SONG

Publish date: 2024-06-01

The good times keep rolling for Judge Faith Jenkins and Kenny Lattimore. The new mom recently took to Instagram with their daughter, Skylar Leigh Lattimore, to celebrate her husband on his chart-topping song.

“There aren’t too many things that can get me out of my baby bubble these days but Kenny’s song becoming #1 in America on @billboard’s adult R&B chart is one of them,” Judge Faith said. I’m SO proud,” she exclaimed.

“Kenny is a master at staying focused, putting his head down, and working! And look at God,” the judge exclaimed. “We put this (and our baby) on our vision board — and I’ve now watched both birthed into reality!”

This year is turning out to be fantastic for the Lattimores. It was just a few weeks ago that Kenny and Faith welcomed their baby girl. Skylar Leigh Lattimore was born to parents who were more than thrilled to meet her for the first time.

“Welcome to the world Skylar Leigh Lattimore,” Faith and Kenny shared with their social media fans. “And just like that life has taken on a whole new meaning,” the celebrity couple revealed.

Life began to take on new meaning for the Lattimores last year. It was during the summer months that Faith and Kenny announced their expectancy of a child. The excited couple used the family dog to help spread the news.

“The last nine months you have supported me in all the ways I needed it most,” Faith told Kenny last month after giving birth. “I watched you juggle a lot with your schedule and you somehow managed to balance it all,” the new mom shared with her husband.

“If Skylar ever wants to know what true love looks like, she will look no further than you,” Faith told Kenny.

Skylar is Faith Jenkins and Kenny Lattimore’s first child together. Kenny released his chart-topping single while preparing for her arrival. Fans of the R&B singer can stream the track on all major music platforms.
