Jeff Wadlow Never Back Down

Wadlow was a director in Never Back Down. Never Back Down was a 2008 American martial arts film directed by Jeff and starring Amber Heard, Cameron Fegreus, Sean Faris, Cam Gigandet, and Djimon Hounsou. It relates the story of a frustrated and clashed teenager who arrives at a new high school and locates an underground fight club there.

The film was theatrically released on March 14, 2008. It obtained typically negative reviews from pundits and grossed $41.6 million worldwide against a budget of $20 million.
