Chris Hemsworth did not eat meat before kissing his vegan Thor co-star Natalie Portman

Publish date: 2024-08-28

In a recent interview, Hollywood stars Natalie Portman and Chris Hemsworth revealed all the fun behind the scenes. The duo worked together in Thor: Love and Thunder, with Portman reprising the role of Jane Foster while Hemsworth played Thor.


The two actors attended a radio show in the UK to promote their movie. During this show, Portman was asked if Chris Hemsworth had any hidden flaws or if the actress had any complaints with Hemsworth while shooting on set. Answering this, Natalie Portman said that she only had pleasant experiences with her co-star.



The actress responded by saying, “He's really nice. He's really a good guy.” She also added, “The day we had a kiss scene he didn't eat meat that morning because I'm vegan. And he eats meat like every half hour. Like, that was so thoughtful. That's not something I'm angry about or care about, but he was just being thoughtful. He's a very nice person.”


As per reports by CNN, Chris Hemsworth eats 4500 calories per day and 10 meals to maintain his physique as the God of Thunder. Natalie Portman, on the other hand, is a vegan. Even though the actress did not insist upon Hemsworth not eating meat, he made the considerate gesture.
