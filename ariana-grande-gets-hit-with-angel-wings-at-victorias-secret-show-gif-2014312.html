Ariana Grande Gets Hit With Angel Wings at Victoria's Secret Show: GIF

Publish date: 2024-02-24

Winging out! Ariana Grande had a “problem” on her hands while performing at the 2014 Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show in London on Tuesday, Dec. 2. Mid-song, the pop sensation, 21, got smacked in the face with a pair of Angel wings worn by model Elsa Hosk.

Grande’s face said it all as she unsuccessfully ducked out of the way. She crouched down and looked up (hilariously!) in horror as Hosk strutted past her. The embarrassment eventually wore off, with Grande even laughing about the situation after her set.

“A Victoria’s Secret angel accidentally smacked me in the face with her wings and it was awesome #goals #bangbangintomyface,” she tweeted Tuesday, giving a shout-out to her song “Bang Bang.”
