Law grew up in Stockport and went to Cheadle Hulme School before attending the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA).

Similarly, she became only the second player in history to go 5–0 and the first from the United Kingdom and Ireland.

Bronte also won the European Ladies Amateur Championship in 2016, making her the world’s number two amateur golfer according to the World Amateur Golf Ranking. In the same way, she participated in the LPGA Final Qualifying Tournament and earned full Symetra Tour status as well as partial LPGA Tour status for 2017.

Interestingly, Law was defeated in a playoff at the LPGA Mediheal Championship by Kim Sei-young in early May 2019. She won the Pure Silk Championship three weeks later, her maiden LPGA Tour triumph.
