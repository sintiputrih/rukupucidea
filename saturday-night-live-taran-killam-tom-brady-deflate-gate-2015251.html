Saturday Night Live's Taran Killam Mocks Tom Brady's &quot;Deflate-Gate&quot;

Publish date: 2024-05-17

Saturday Night Live goes pro! Opening the sketch comedy show on Jan. 24 was a skit surrounding the recent New England Patriots “Deflate-gate” scandal that has been widely blamed on quarterback Tom Brady. Longtime SNL cast member Taran Killam played the athlete, beanie and all, claiming innocence and stupidity. 

“Me? Absolutely not!” Killam replied when a reporter asked him if he deflated the footballs. “Honestly I wasn’t even aware that footballs couldn’t be inflated or deflated. All I know is that a football is a pigskin, so I just assumed that the air in the football is how much air is inside the pig when it died.”

Brady held his own real-life press conference on Thursday, Jan. 22, continuing to claim he did not alter the footballs in his AFC Championship Game against the Indianapolis Colts. The match secured the Patriots a spot in the 2015 Super Bowl against the Seattle Seahawks.
