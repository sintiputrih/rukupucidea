What weight class is Ryan Garcia?

Publish date: 2024-04-15

Ryan Garcia, a prominent American professional boxer, has showcased his talents in several weight classes throughout his career.

Current Weight Class

As of now, Ryan Garcia competes in the lightweight division. Standing at a height of 5 feet 8.5 inches with a reach of 70 inches, Garcia has established himself as a formidable presence in the lightweight category.

His statistics reflect a remarkable boxing record, with 25 wins, 20 of which were achieved by knockout, and 1 loss.

Past Weight Class Changes

Despite currently competing in the lightweight division, Ryan Garcia has previously displayed his versatility by fighting in multiple weight classes.

He has also competed in the super featherweight and light welterweight categories, showcasing his adaptability and skill across different weight divisions. 

His ability to transition between weight classes underscores his agility and determination as a professional boxer.

Notable Fights and Achievements

Ryan Garcia's career is adorned with significant victories and remarkable achievements in the lightweight division.

In a notable fight against Devin Haney, Garcia emerged victorious, showcasing his prowess and solidifying his standing in the lightweight category. 

His notable wins over Jayson Velez, Luke Campbell, and Javier Fortuna have contributed to his reputation as a top talent in the lightweight division.
