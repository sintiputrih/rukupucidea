Lauren LaVera is one of the best American actresses who is best known for playing her role in the Netflix TV series i.e., Marvel’s Iron Fist. Outside the Netflix world, she is known for the role she played in the crime drama Wetlands (2017) alongside other famous celebrities i.e., Heather Graham and Adewale Akinnuoye-Agbaje. Later, she was casted in Clinton Road (2019) horror movie as a leading role opposite Ice-T, Eric Roberts, and Vincent Pastore.

Later in the year 2021, she played a role of a daughter in the Gilly and Keeves (TV series) as Tana. In the series, Shane Gillis is on-screen dad and Kilah Fox is on-screen mother of Lauren LaVera.

As of writing this article, Lauren just finished working on a horror movie name Terrifier 2 and it is released on October 6, 2022.
