Sofia Vergara's Best Red Carpet Moments

Publish date: 2024-10-02

Out of all the celebrities in Hollywood, Modern Family alum Sofia Vergara has one of the most signature styles. Though the Colombian actress varies in color and neckline for each event, Vergara has proved she knows what she likes: figure-hugging dresses that show off her curves.

Related story Keira Knightley’s Super-Rare Public Appearance Shows She’s Forever a Chanel It-Girl

Talking to Net-a-Porter back in 2016, Vergara explained why she’s developed such a staple look. “People will often say that I wear the same thing on the red carpet, but I know my body: it’s very voluptuous and I’ve got the boobs of a stripper,” she revealed, adding that having triple D breasts often limits her fashion options. “I need my dresses to have structure – and under armor.”

“There is so much going on under my dresses that I bleed at the end of award ceremonies,” she continued. “In ten years I think it would be good to have a reduction. I don’t think it’s even going to be an option not to have surgery, because I’m going to start having back pains. I wouldn’t make them too small – just enough that I don’t end up looking like an old stripper.”

Although we can’t say we totally agree with Vergara’s stripper comparison, any large-chested people out there understand how difficult it is to shop for your body and feel good in it too. And, looking at Vergara’s many looks over the years, “good” is just an understatement. Take a look at all of her best looks below!

A version of this article was originally published on July 2023. 

  • After-Party Ready

    Vergara brought her sultry style to the 2024 Vanity Fair Oscar Party in this black floor-length gown.

  • Gold for ‘Griselda’

    In Jan 2024, Sofía Vergara dazzled in this metallic gown, which is a custom Laura Basci, for the premiere of Griselda.

  • Sparkling Jumpsuit Era

    For the 2023 Albies, Sofia Vergara arrived in this showstopping, sparkling black jumpsuit that turned every single head!

  • Rocking Black & White

    Vergara, who usually doesn’t opt for patterns, chose for this chic plaid black and white look for the Carol Burnett: 90 Years of Laughter + Love event. 

  • Yellow Power

    Vergara proved that less is more at 2022’s 74th Annual Primetime Emmy Awards in this bright yellow spaghetti-strap dress with sparce red sequins. 

  • Jumpsuit Time!

    On very rare occasions, Vergara opts for something other than a dress. At the 2022 Nickelodeon’s Kids’ Choice Awards, for example, she changed it up and rocked this cream-colored one-shoulder jumpsuit. 

  • Powerful in Red

    Vergara looked gorgeous in this beaded maroon dress with a feathered trim at America’s Got Talent‘s Season 17 Kick-Off Red Carpet.

  • Classic in Champagne

    Vergara looked like a vision at the 2022 Vanity Fair Oscar Party in this champagne and gray strapless dress that flattered her curves in all the right ways. 

  • Babe in Barbie-core

    Long before Barbie was in the works, Vergara embraced Barbie pink in this figure-hugging dress at the America’s Got Talent Season 16 Live Shows

  • Art Deco Vibes

    At the 2020 Vanity Fair Oscar Party, Vergara looked beautiful in this intricately-designed black strapless dress with silver sequins that gave major Art Deco vibes. 

  • Sexy in Lace

    Although Vergara’s a big fan of color, she sometimes surprises us by rocking a carpet in all-black. At the premiere of Jay & Silent Bob Reboot, Vergara did just that in this black dress with lace details and a sheer overlay. 

  • All About the Layers

    Vergara steered clear of her classic figure-hugging dresses at the premiere of Solo: A Star Wars Story in this chic layered dress with handwriting details all over it. 

  • Fancy in Floral

    As a Courage Award recipient at WCRF’s “An Unforgettable Evening,” Vergara looked as sophisticated as ever in this black cocktail dress with a colorful floral pattern.

  • Businesswoman Chic

    Speaking of floral, Vergara rocked this blazer and matching skirt combo with red flower appliqués to the L.A. Italia Fest Film Fest back in 2018. 

  • Trumpet-Style

    At the 2017 Primetime Emmy Awards, Vergara was all smiles as she posed on the red carpet in this stunning strapless white dress with a trumpet-style voluminous bottom. 

  • Golden Girl

    Vergara commanded everyone’s attention at the 2017 Golden Globe Awards in this intricate gold and silver lace gown with sheer cutouts. 

  • Head-Turning in Navy

    In another head-turning moment, Vergara looked gorgeous at the 88th Annual Academy Awards in 2016 in this navy ruched dress that cinched her curves in all the right ways.  

  • Radiant in Rose Gold

    Vergara opted for a more modern look at the 68th Annual Primetime Emmy Awards in this rose gold dress with asymmetrical sequined details that made the look cool and modern.

  • Pop of Pink

    Months before, at the 22nd Annual Screen Actors Guild Awards, Vergara opted for a totally different vibe in this simple yet ultra-vibrant bright pink column dress. One of our favorites to date!

  • Vision in Blue

    At the 2015 Vanity Fair Oscar Party, Vergara looked gorgeous in this strapless blue gown with silver embroidery all throughout. 

  • Always Angelic

    At 2015’s Met Gala, which had “China: Through The Looking Glass” as the theme, Vergara looked like the definition of angelic in this white strapless dress with an ultra-flowy skirt. 

  • One-Shoulder Wonder

    At the 2015 Screen Actors Guild Awards, Vergara wowed in this red one-shoulder dress with a corseted detail on the bodice. 

  • Bringing the Volume

    At the 2014 Golden Globe Awards, Vergara wasn’t afraid to take up the whole carpet in this ultra-poofy black strapless dress. She then paired the dress with a statement blue necklace, the perfect choice!

  • Winning White

    Vergara showed just how figure-flattering corseted dress can be in this white gown with silver detail at the 2014 Annual Primetime Emmy Awards.

  • Bringing Sexy Back

    While posing at the 2014 Annual Screen Actors Guild Awards, Vergara showed off her silver sequined dress’ best feature: the sexy low back. 

  • Sultry in Sequins

    A few days earlier, at the 71st Annual Golden Globe Awards After Party, Vergara wowed in another silver gown this time with a plunging neckline. 

  • Matching the Carpet

    Vergara perfectly matched the red carpet in this bright red lace dress with a mermaid silhouette at the 2013 Primetime Emmy Awards. 

  • Green Goddess

    Vergara proved she’s once again not afraid of bright colors in this bright green dress at the 2013 CFDA Fashion Awards.

  • Giving Greek

    Vergara looked like a Greek goddess at the 2013 Screen Actors Guild Awards in this ivory-colored draped dress with a one-shoulder halter neckline. 

  • Owning Mermaidcore

    In one of our favorite looks of hers from all time, Vergara looked absolutely incredible at the 2012 Primetime Emmy Awards in this sequined teal dress by Zuhair Murad. 

  • Pretty in Peplum

    At the 2012 Met Gala, which had the theme “Schiaparelli And Prada: Impossible Conversations,” Vergara looked regal in this silver strapless gown with a peplum silhouette. 

  • Loving the Lace

    Vergara embraced her sexy and sultry side in this sheer black lace dress at the 2011 Vanity Fair Oscar Party. 

  • Elegant in Emerald

    While attending the 16th Annual Elton John AIDS Foundation Oscar Party in 2008, Vergara turned heads in this plunging emerald gown.
