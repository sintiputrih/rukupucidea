Shawn Wayans’s Character Ray Wilkins Exudes Gay Energy

In the 2000 satire film Scary Movie, Wayans played Ray Wilkins. Despite the fact that his character was not identified as gay, many assumed he was.

Ray chose to appear gay in a locker room scenario at one point. He asked his pal if the yellow vest he was wearing made him look gay as he changed his shirt. When his companion said no, he pulled the bottom of his vest inside and out to draw attention to his chest and asked whether it was enough.

In another episode, he requested that his girlfriend Brenda dress as a guy before they had sex. Ray was also supportive of one of his male pals as if that wasn’t enough. He gave him flowers, ran his bathwater, and even slept next to him on restless nights.

In a nutshell, Ray was shown as a man who claimed to be straight yet acted like a gay man. Many people assumed Wayans was gay because of his portrayal.

Shawn has three children with Ursula Alberto, his baby mama.

Wayans’ long-term relationship is one fact that dispels LGBT accusations. Ursula Alberto, a fitness guru, was with him. They were together from 1999 through 2008, despite never marrying.

They have three children as a result of their relationship. Laila, their first child from their marriage, was born in 1999. The second child, Ilia, was born in 2002, followed by Marlon Wayans in 2004. Four years after the birth of their last child, the couple divorced. The Little Man star hasn’t been in a public relationship since his divorce from his baby mama.

Shawn Wayans Is Not Gay, Claims Brother

People speculated that Wayans was gay because of his portrayal in the film Scary Movie.
But he wasn’t the only one caught up in the rumor mill. Marlon Wayans, his younger brother and a member of the Scary Movie franchise, was also rumored to be gay. Marlon was seen leaving a club with a transsexual person in 2010, prompting the stories.

Marlon responded to the question about his and his brother’s orientation on Twitter. He refuted the allegations, insisting that he and his brother were both heterosexual.

Furthermore, in 2015, the actor announced his relationship status and debunked gay rumors once more. Shawn said that he was seeing a nurse during an interview with Radio 99.7’s homosexual DJs Fernando and Greg.

When the hosts asked if he was always the one who had to pay for the dates, he stated that his girlfriend treated him well on occasion because she was doing well in her work.

But that was the extent of his relationship discussion. But, for the time being, the Shawn Wayans gay rumors may be put to rest. Simple guesses on someone’s orientation are often frowned upon in the absence of verifiable confirmation.

Where is Shawn Wayans Now?

Wayans is on the road as of August 2021. In January, he announced the dates and cities of his Shawn Wayans Comedy Tour on Instagram.

His last scheduled performance is at the San Jose Improv in San Jose, California, on August 8, 2021. After the COVID-19 pandemic limitations in the United States were relaxed, he launched his comedy tour.

The comic appeared to be ready to resume his tour, based on his caption. He encouraged fans to come out and have a good time.
