The 10 Coolest Superhero Girlfriends

Publish date: 2024-08-24

Whether you prefer superheroes from Marvel Comics or DC Comics, what mostly all superheroes have in common is that they usually have a love interest! Some of them have girlfriends, some of them have wives, and some of them experience short-lived flings. In some cases, superheroes have a girlfriend who also has superpowers of her own but in a lot of other instances, a superhero ends up with a girlfriend who finds herself being a damsel in distress all the time and in need of a rescue.

According to traditional comic books, some of the most incredible superhero girlfriends in history have been supportive, loving matches for the bravest superheroes ever who risk their lives all the time to fight against villains and bad guys.

10 Lois Lane - Superman's Love Interest

Superman is one of the most iconic superheroes ever so it makes sense that his love interest is just as iconic as him. Everyone knows the name Lois Lane. She is a little bit of a busy-body because she is a reporter who has a nose for news... but that doesn’t mean that she isn’t an awesome love interest for Superman. She cares about exposing the truth and telling stories about what is going on in the world around her and she is constantly on a pursuit for justice. She is an incredible match for Superman.

9 Jane Foster - Thor's Love Interest

Thor’s love interest is named Jane Foster. In the movies, she is played by none other than the Natalie Portman. Natalie Portman does a great job taking on the role of Jane Foster. Jane Foster existed in the comic books and it was great to see her come to life in the thrilling Marvel movies as well. The first time the character of Jane Foster ever appeared it was in a comic book called Journey into Mystery #84. She will be taking on the mantle of Thor in the upcoming Thor: Love and Thunder.

8 Jean Grey - Cyclops' Love Interest

Jean Grey and Cyclops have an interesting love story. In the comic books, she is also romantically linked to Wolverine but in the movies, viewers are able to watch her relationship with Cyclops be the focal point of her story. They have very different powers but that doesn’t take away from the bond that they share. According to the comic books, they actually get married at one point and during their honeymoon, they are able to visit the future! Jean Grey is also famously one of the most powerful members of the X-Men.

7 Mera - Aquaman's Love Interest

Aquaman's love interest is Mera. The first Aquaman movie premiered in 2018 and is classified as both an action and adventure movie. It stars Jason Momoa as Aquaman himself. As for who plays Mera, his love interest? Amber Heard. Her reputation in the public eye isn’t too great right now due to her scandalous divorce from Johnny Depp but the character she plays is pretty amazing. Mera is an awesome love interest for Aquaman. The character of Mera has the power to harden water into solid objects to use to her advantage.

6 Iris West - The Flash's Love Interest

The Flash has an incredible love interest and her name is Iris West. In fact, they become much more than just mutual love interests… They actually get married. The relationship between the two is an awesome one to see and on their CW show, the actress they cast to play the role of Iris is African-American. The interracial relationship between the two was a big deal for viewers everywhere! Iris West has the ability to swap people's DNA.

5 Vanessa Carlysle - Deadpool's Love Interest

Deadpool’s love interest is Vanessa Carlysle. She was once a lady of the night who worked street corners but she changed her way of life to be with Deadpool.

Even after his skin got messed up and he didn’t look the same way that he visually looked before, she didn’t change her mind about him. She still remained in love with him regardless. Talk about loyalty! Will we be seeing her in a third movie?

4 Mary Jane Watson - Spider-Man's Love Interest

The relationship between Mary Jane Watson and Spider-Man is one that many teenagers wish they could emulate. She is the girl he has a crush on at his high school and he pines after her for a long time until they are finally able to be together. He has a lot more confidence and self-esteem when he is Spider-Man, rather than Peter Parker, so that is when he feels more bold and comfortable spending time with her. At least for a little while.

3 Nakia - Black Panther's Love Interest

Black Panther, who was portrayed by the late Chadwick Boseman, has two love interests according to comic books. One of them is Storm from X-Men and the other one is Nakia.

RELATED: These Are Black Panther Chadwick Boseman's Most Inspirational Quotes

Nakia is the love interest viewers get to see him try to woo in the movies so she is the love interest we are going to focus on here and now! Not only is Nakia portrayed as fiercely loyal and strong and battle, she is also shown to be independent when she turns down T'Challa's advances.

2 Invisible Woman - Mister Fantastic's Love Interest

Mister Fantastic is married to Invisible Woman. A member of the Fantastic Four in her own right, the two have always been seen as relationship goals. Her superpowers alone make her impressive and enviable. She can turn invisible whenever she wants to and she can create force fields to protect her self and those around her. A lot of comic book fans disapprove of this relationship because of the way Mister Fantastic treats her but at the end of the day, she stays loyal and remains by his side.

1 Pepper Potts - Iron Man's Love Interest

Pepper Potts is obviously one of the best superhero love interests ever. She went from being Tony Stark’s assistant to his CEO and eventually his wife! There is no one else that he could ever trust as much as he trusts Pepper Potts. She proved herself time and time again to always have his back and always have his best interest at heart. At one point, she even put on a super-suit herself!
