Sheri Moon Zombie-Biography

Sheri Moon Zombie is an American actress, dancer, model, and fashion designer best known for her role as Heidi in The Lords of Salem, a supernatural horror film. She has also appeared in films such as The Devil’s Rejects, Halloween, 3 From Hell, Californication, and CSI: Miami.
