Pierre Garcon net worth: Pierre Garcon is an American professional football player who has a net worth of $16 million. Pierre Garcon was born in Putnam County, New York in August 1986. He was drafted #205 by the Indianapolis Colts in the 2008 NFL Draft and played there until 2011. Garcon has played for the Washington Redskins since 2012.
