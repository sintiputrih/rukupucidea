Tampa Bay Buccaneers – 74

The two-time Super Bowl champions (XXVII and LV) are expected to have 74 overall in the 2024’s version of the game.

Last year, they got 92; this year, it is expected to drop drastically without Tom Brady.

The QB position is up for grabs. Kyle Trask will compete with Baker Mayfield for the starting role. Playing with the Buccs can make you feel incompetent without a star QB.

RT Tristan Wirfs is supposed to get 92, while MLB Lavonte David and WR Mike Evans may get 91 and 90. Likewise, they have other quality players like C Ryan Jensen, DT Vita Vea, FS Antonio Winfield Jr., and WR Chris Godwin.

Dallas Cowboys – 79

Dallas Cowboys get a 79 overall. It should be heavily credited to its top-notch athletes, RG Zack Martin and ROLB Micah Parsons.

WR CeeDee Lamb is anticipated to get 90, along with CB Stephon Gilmore, LE DeMarcus Lawrence, and LT Tyron Smith. Both offense and defense look solid on the paper.

The interior defensive line poses questions as Mazi Smith will play his rookie year. Osa Odigghizuwa, Quinton Bohanna, Jonathan Hankins, and Neville Gallimore do not have explosiveness and durability.

Green Bay Packers – 76

Green Bay Packers without Aaron Rodgers is now a reality in 2023. The 2024 edition of the game will see the QB don the Jets uniform.

CB Jaire Alexander, LOLB Rashan Gary, and DT Kenny Clark are crucial defensive players. LT David Bakhtiari, HB Aaron Jones, and LG Elgton Jenkins make up the pillars of offense.

If they had experienced hands in the receiving end at the spread, they would be standout as favorites for the gamers.

Los Angeles Rams – 73

The 2021 NFL winners will indeed have the best RE in the form of Aaron Donald in the game. He is expected to make the 99 Club.

The experienced WR Cooper Kupp, WR Can Jefferson, HB Cam Ackers, and QB Matthew Stafford all have a Super Bowl ring.

Playing with this without Donald and Ernest Jones will have many holes in the defense. If they are out of stamina, you are bound to struggle to keep the opponent down.

Arizona Cardinals – 72

Arizona Cardinals had 84 in the previous edition, while we can expect them to be one of the weakest, with 72 in the latest installment of the game.

SS Budda Baker is one of the best in his positions. He is sure to land 90 or above.

Likewise, WR Marquise Brown, TE Zach Ertz, SS Isaiah Simmons, and WR Rondale Moore are other magnificent players in the franchise.

Best Offensive Playbook in Madden 24

Madden 24 best offensive playbook to use is as follows:

Kansas City Chiefs Offensive Playbook

It is designed to open spaces for Tight Ends. The pass-oriented strategy helps in placing receivers in crucial positions.

Two WRs and TEs power it. You will also need one RB to bring down the opponent’s defense.

It does not suit a rushing play. Instead, those comfortable building from the back and throwing short and long passes can benefit.

These formations are the most effective in this lot:

  • Gun Wing Flex Offset WK
  • Pistol Wing Flex Close
  • Gun Deuce Close
  • Gun Trio Offset Wk

Baltimore Ravens Offensive Playbook

Those who like to run the ball will indeed consider it a heaven. The best rushing plays with incredible tricks and strategy is its main highlight.

A dual-threat QB will benefit from this offense style as you can rush him to gain yards.

Apart from the running play, it has some quality pass and run options, mainly focusing on TEs. You can try I Form Twin TE, Split Wing Nasty, Empty Bae Flex, or Strong I Wing.

Detroit Lions Offensive Playbook

It has a blend of passing and running approach. The formations mainly help you throw deep and gain yards, but occasionally, you can surprise the opponent with a running play.

Letting your rival keep guessing your next move will work in your favor.

Implement this play style with three WRs and one RB and TE. You can carry out the Gun Doubles, I Form Slot Close, Singleback Wing Stack, or Gun Trips TE.

Best Defense to Run in Madden 24

Madden 24 best defense to run is:

Philadelphia Eagles

Adding Jalen Carter in the 2023 NFL Draft makes them a dominant defensive unit. Nolan Smith will prove to be another worthy fellow in the squad.

The presence of Darius Slay and James Bradbury will relieve the new Defensive Coordinator, Sean Desai. The duo helped the team register 70 sacks in 2022. It was the only fourth time a franchise had reached that number.

They have also added Syndey Brown to strengthen the Safety position.

San Francisco 49ers

The 49ers only allowed 77.7 rushing yards per game in 2022. Their opponent only managed to gain 3.4 yards per carry.

A proven defensive coordinator like Steve Wilks can work wonders with Javon Hargrave and Ji’Ayir Brown.

Likewise, the presence of Nick Mosa, Arik Armstead, Fred Warner, and Drake Jackson is enough to turn the opponent’s day into a nightmare.

Dallas Cowboys

The trio of Dan Quinn, Micah Parsons, and Trevon Diggs wreaked havoc in 2022. They were top-notch in all defensive metrics.

The inclusion of Stephon Gilmore adds quality to the side. Mazi Smith is expected to enhance the defense further and is seen as a long-term player for the franchise.

DeMarcus Lawrence and Jayron Kearse must play their parts well for the defense to click immediately.
