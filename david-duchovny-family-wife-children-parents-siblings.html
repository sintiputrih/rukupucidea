David Duchovny family, wife, children, parents, siblings

Publish date: 2024-09-21

David Duchovny is an American actor, writer, producer and musician who is loved by many following his great personality and talent.

As an actor, he portrayed FBI agent Fox Mulder on the television series The X-Files and played the writer Hank Moody on the television series Californication, both of which have earned him Golden Globe awards.

This article will dive into his personal life and family to know his wife, children, parents, and siblings.

Table of Content

David Duchovny wife

David is a divorcee who has been married once. He was married to actress, Téa Leoni. They were married from 1997 to 2014.

David Duchovny children

He is the father of two children – a daughter born in April 1999 and a son born in June 2002. His children are actress West Duchovny and her brother, Kyd Miller Duchovny.

David Duchovny parents

David is the son of an American non-fiction writer, playwright and novelist who is known for Coney Island Kid, Coney and The Billion Dollar Swindle: Frauds Against the Elderly, Amram Ducovny and a teacher, and school administrator, Margaret Ducovny.

His father is no more since he passed away on August 23, 2003, in Paris, France.

David Duchovny siblings

He is one of three children of his parents. He happens to be the middle and second child of his parents, hence, he has an older brother, Daniel Duchovny and a younger sister who has been identified as Laurie Duchovny.
