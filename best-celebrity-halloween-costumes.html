Rita Ora, Anne Hathaway, Kate Moss: The 25 best celebrity Halloween costumes you probably forgot abo

Publish date: 2024-08-21

When it comes to dressing up for Halloween, the rule of thumb seems to be go big or go home. At least that's the case in point for our favourite style icons, who over the years have served some of the most memorable spooky season looks. 

Whether you're a first time Halloween goer or a seasoned pro when it comes to donning a costume, we gathered our favourite Halloween looks of all time to inspire your next outfit.

Lili Reinhart, Madelaine Petsch and Camila Mendes - Hocus Pocus

The Riverdale stars and close friends Lili, Madelaine, and Camila absolutely aced the task with their costumes inspired by the Sanderson sisters from Hocus Pocus in 2022.

Megan Fox - Pamela Anderson

Megan Fox delivered a stunning portrayal of Pamela Anderson, giving Lily James tough competition. To add to the excitement, her fiancé Machine Gun Kelly also joined in, taking on the role of Tommy Lee.

Kylie Jenner - Bride of Frankenstein

The Bride of Frankenstein look created by the beauty mogul was absolutely iconic. It was truly legendary, especially considering the extra effort put into organizing a complete photoshoot featuring a creepy laboratory setting.

Emily Ratajkowski - Marge Simpson

Emily Ratajkowski fully embraced the cartoon look as Marge from The Simpsons donning a tall, blue beehive hairstyle with yellow body paint to match the character's skin tone at Heidi Klum's Halloween Party in 2015. 

Rita Ora - Post Malone

Rita Ora attended the KISS Haunted House Party at The SSE Arena, Wembley in 2018 dressed as Post Malone with his signature face tattoos, rings, and confidence. The finishing touch? A bucketful of swagger.

Cardi B - Evil Nun

Rapper, songwriter, actress and TV personality Cardi B performed at the 'Demon Dome' opening celebration on Halloween at KAOS Dayclub & Nightclub in 2019 dressed as a sinister nun, accessorising with a creepy cross necklace.

Bella Hadid - Lydia Deetz

Supermodel Bella Hadid and her then-boyfriend The Weeknd were seen leaving Heidi Klum's 19th Annual Halloween Party in 2018 dressed as Lydia Deetz and Beetlejuice.

Kim Kardashian & Jonathan Cheban - Sonny & Cher

Kim Kardashian and pal Jonathan Cheban attended the Casamigos Halloween Party in 2017 dressed as Sonny and Cher. The Skims founder totally nailed it - right down to her poker straight hair and all-eyes-on-me lash look.

Kendall Jenner - Fembot

Kendall Jenner attended the Casamigos Halloween party at CATCH Las Vegas at ARIA Resort & Casino in 2018 in Las Vegas dressed as a Fembot from Austin Powers.

Katy Perry - A Cheeto

Katy Perry was seen at Kate Hudson's annual Halloween party in 2014 in Los Angeles, California dressed as a cheeto. Iconic party guest behaviour if ever we saw it.

Harry Styles - Elton John

Harry Styles attended the Casamigos Halloween Party in 2018 covered in rhinestones, paying tribute to Elton John.

Gigi Hadid - Sandy from Grease

Gigi Hadid attended the Heidi Klum Halloween Party in 2015 in New York City dressed as Sandy from Grease.

Zoë Kravitz - Vampire

In 2018, Zoë Kravitz was snapped on her way to the Casamigos Halloween party in an outfit that she called "Mornings Suck Vampire." 

Anne Hathaway - Cleopatra

Actress Anne Hathaway attended Heidi Klum's 5th Annual Halloween party at Marquee in 2004 in New York City dressed as Queen of the Nile, Cleopatra.

Lupita Nyong'o - Dionne from Clueless

Clad in plaid, actress Lupita Nyong'o was a 90s dream when she attended Heidi Klum's 19th Annual Halloween Party in 2018 dressed as Dionne from Clueless. 

Nicky Hilton Rothschild - Paris Hilton

Nicky Hilton Rothschild attended the Casamigos Halloween Party in 2018 dressed as her sister Paris Hilton. That's hot. 

Martha Hunt - Leeloo from The Fifth Element

Model Martha Hunt attended Heidi Klum's 20th Annual Halloween Party presented by Amazon Prime Video and SVEDKA Vodka at Cathédrale New York in 2019 dressed as Leeloo from The Fifth Element.

Kate Moss - Cara Delevingne

Kate Moss dressed as Cara Delevingne to attend the 'Death Of A Geisha' party hosted by Fran Cutler and Cafe KaiZen with Grey Goose in 2014. 

Lucy Boynton - Corpse Bride

Lucy Boynton attended Bette Midler's 2019 Hulaween at New York Hilton Midtown in 2019 dressed as Corpse Bride. 

 Winnie Harlow - RuPaul

Model Winnie Harlow was snapped at Heidi Klum's 19th Annual Halloween Party at Lavo NYC in 2018 dressed as drag royalty RuPaul. 

Heidi Klum - Lady Godiva

Model Heidi Klum arrived on horseback as Lady Godiva at her Annual Halloween Party at Lot 61 in New York back in 2001.

Elsa Hosk - Alice from Closer

Elsa Hosk attended Heidi Klum's 20th Annual Halloween Party in 2019 in New York dressed as Natalie Portman's character Alice in Closer.

 Princess Olympia & Ella Richards - The Grady twins

Princess Maria-Olympia of Greece and Denmark and Ella Richards attended Annabel's Annual Halloween Party 'Circus of Horrors' in 2021 dressed as the Grady girls, aka the twins from 1980 horror classic The Shining.

Emma Weymouth - Ice Queen

The Marchioness of Bath, Emma Weymouth, dressed as the Ice Queen for a Halloween party hosted by Annabel's in 2016. 

Jemima Khan - Melania Trump

Jemima Khan attended the Unicef UK Halloween Ball in 2016 dressed as Melania Trump.
