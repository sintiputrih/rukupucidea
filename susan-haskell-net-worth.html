Susan Haskell is a Canadian actress. Her professional acting career started in the 1990s. During this period, she made her debut as Dr. Margaret “Marty” Saybrooke on the soap opera One Life to Live. She first starred on the show from 1992 to 1997. She returned as a guest star in the 2000s. At the same time, she also guest-starred in an episode of Port Charles. From 1998 to 2001, she became further known for her recurring character as Lt. Cmdr. Jordan Parker on the NBC series JAG. Afterward, she had a one-time role on Crossing Jordan. In 2006, she made her cinematic debut in the film The Good Shepherd.
