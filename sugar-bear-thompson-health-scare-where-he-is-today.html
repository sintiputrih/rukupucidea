What Has Happened To Sugar Bear Thompson's Personal Life?

When most fans think of Sugar Bear Thompson, it is the fact that he is Alana "Honey Boo Boo" Thompson's father that comes to mind first and foremost. Of course, by definition, that means that Sugar Bear was involved with Mama June at one time.

What Caused Sugar Bear Thompson's Dental Emergency?

Many decades ago, some doctors actually believed that tobacco was good for people. Over the last several decades, however, that certainly has changed as virtually everyone now knows that tobacco is awful for people.

Fortunately, tobacco usage has significantly lessened over the last several years. However, even though it is wonderful that many people have quit using tobacco products, some of them will still suffer consequences from their past dependence.

After Sugar Bear Thompson regularly used chewing tobacco for more than a decade, he eventually put the habit behind him. Still, in May 2022, it seemed like Sugar Bear's past tobacco usage had caught up with him.

Following the end of his marriage to Jennifer, Sugar Bear wanted to look more attractive. As a result, Sugar Bear sought to get his teeth fixed in May 2022 and that led to his dentist finding something very troubling.

While examining Sugar Bear's mouth, his dentist discovered several white spots on the Here Comes Honey Boo Boo star's gums. As doctors and dentists have figured out over the years, spots like those often are an indication of cancer.

While there is no such thing as a good kind of cancer, there certainly are forms of cancer that are worse than others. Tragically for anyone who contracts them, cancers of the throat and mouth are extremely difficult to battle.

Fortunately for Sugar Bear, it turns out that he isn't one of many celebrities who've beaten cancer. The reason for that is while a tumor was removed from Sugar Bear's mouth, it was benign.

After Sugar Bear's benign tumor was removed, his dental makeover could proceed as previously planned. After Sugar Bear's teeth were removed, his dentist installed top and bottom snap-in dentures.

To say that Sugar Bear's new teeth transformed his face is an understatement. Sugar Bear also underwent other procedures including a hair transplant and injections to get wrinkles removed from his face.
