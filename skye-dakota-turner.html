Skye Dakota Turner is an American actor, theatre artist, singer, and musician who rose to fame with her portrayal of young Aretha Franklin in the 2021 biographical musical drama film Respect, based on the life of American singer, songwriter, and pianist Aretha Franklin. Skye has also been a part of projects such as The Late Show with Stephen Colbert, Tamron Hall, and Good Morning America.
