Ariel And Her Sisters Live Action Looks and Actors

Publish date: 2024-02-06

Ariel Sisters names in Live Action are Mala, Indira, Caspia, Tamika, Karina, and Perla. Ariel has 6 sisters in The Little Mermaid 2023.

The Little Mermaid is a revised version of the 1989 animated film. The film tells the story of the youngest daughter of King Triton Ariel.

The film will be released worldwide on May 26, 2023. The movie is directed by Rob Marshal and the screenplay by David Magee.

In the musical fantasy film, Ariel is the princess of the Kingdom of Atlantica. She is extremely fascinated with the human world and wants to know about it.

As the story progresses, she falls in love with Prince Eric after saving him from a sinking ship. She could not help herself and became determined to be with him.

When her father, King Triton got to know about this, he went against her wish which led Ariel to the sea witch Ursula and make a deal with her.

Her love for Prince Eric and stubborn nature ultimately places her life and her father’s crown in danger.

Some of the cast of the Disney movies are Halle Bailey, Jonah Hauer-King, Melissa McCarthy, Javier Bardem, Noma Dumezweni, and Daveed Diggs.

Ariel Sisters Live Action Looks Revealed

Ariel and Sisters live action looks were revealed before the movie’s big release. In The Little Mermaid movie, all the Ariel sisters are from diverse backgrounds.

The fans of the movie were happy with the changes in the name of Ariel’s sisters. They found it a more interesting as the new names are easy-to-remember.

Similarly, as the release date is approaching near, fans cannot wait to see the new as well as a revised character on the big screen.

Ariel sisters names live-action are Perla, Indira, Karina, Caspia, Mala, and Tamika.

1. Perla

Perla is a tall fair looking mermaid with braided hair and red and whitetails. She is referred to as the princess of Atlantica.

The Mermaid character Perla is based on Andrina from the original The Little Mermaid Film.

Perla is the sixth daughter of King Triton, She is the older sister to Ariel and the youngest of four sisters.

2. Indira

Indira is a long-haired dark colored mermaid with a yellow mermaid tail. She is also one of Ariel’s Mermaid-sisters.

The Character Indira is based on Alana from the original Animated Mermaid movie.

Indira is the second born of the family and is the younger sister to Mala and older to Caspia, Tamika, Karina, Perla, and Ariel.

3. Karina

Karina is a beautiful Mermaid with a blue tail with white ponytail hair.

The Mermaid Karina is based on Arista from the original The Little Mermaid Film.

Karina is the fifth-born daughter of King Triton and the older sister to Perla and Ariel.

4. Caspia

Caspia is a beautiful pink hair Mermaid with a dark blue and orange tail.

Caspia is based on the character Adella from the original Mermaid movie.

Caspia is the third Mermaid daughter of the King of Atlantica. She is the youngest sister of Mala and Indira.

5. Mala

Mala is an Asian-inspired beautiful mermaid with long hair and a pink mermaid tail.

Mala is the new version of Aquata from the original old mermaid movie. She is the fourth-born daughter of King Triton.

Mala is the firstborn of King Triton and Queen Athena.

6. Tamika

Tamika is a beautiful dark-skinned Mermaid with afro-style hair and a mixed-colored mermaid tail.

She is the middle daughter of King Triton and the older sister to Karina, Perla, and Ariel.

She is based on Attina from the original Little Mermaid movie.

How Many Sisters Does Ariel Have?

Ariel has six older sisters in both the 1989 animated movie and in 2023 Little Mermaid live-action. The seven sisters represent the seven seas.

In the 1989 Little Mermaid movie, the seven sisters are the daughters of Triton and the late Queen Athena, King of Atlantica.

They were referred to as the daughters of Triton and the seven princesses of the kingdom of Atlantica.

All of his daughter’s names started with the A initial in the original animated The Little Mermaid movie.

The names of King Triton’s seven daughters are Attina, Alana, Adella, Aquata, Arista, Andrina, and Ariel.

King Tritton of Atlantica kept all his daughter name after his wife, Queen Athena in the past version of the movie.

Per Redditor, it was extremely difficult to recognize their names and identify each character from their look as everyone looked similar in the original film.

However, they are happy that the new film decided to change the name of the little mermaid and kept it simple than before.

Another important thing in the remake version of the movie is the characters are diverse and look realistic.

As per the Redditor, the 1989 version’s name sound similar as it was cartoonized and now the new name makes sense and sounds good for a live-action movie.

All of Ariel’s sisters’ roles in the movie are minor, as the movie is focused on the lead character Ariel and her part of the story.

The mermaid sisters only get limited time on the screen. However, fans are eager to see all of them sharing the screen together.

Who Plays Ariel Sisters in The Little Mermaid 2023?

The live-action remake of Disney’s The Little Mermaid has made various changes when compared to the 1989 animated movie.

The filmmakers have modernized several characters and have given a twist to the story as per the need.

The audience will soon experience the change in character as well as the story after seeing it on the big screen.

The new remake live-action movie is said to have incorporated some of today’s sensibilities into the timeless characters.

At first, when they dropped the trailer of the movie, there was major criticism for the changes in character from its original version.

The main role Ariel played by Halle Bailey was also criticized by the audience as the original animated movie’s Ariel was fair skin with bright red hair.

There was also a fair share of debate about Scuttle and Sebastian’s characters, there are various threads of debates on social media.

The fans were angry that in the name of the remake, the filmmaker are changing the whole authenticity of the movie.

In the original film, Scuttle is a seagull who helps Ariel to find items for her collectible and gives her insight into the human land.

However, in the remake movie, Scuttle is a female northern gannet, they change the bird as the real Scuttle was not able to do the underwater scene.

While the original Scuttle was a beach bird who couldn’t swim under the water. The creators wanted to make characters as diverse as they could.

Similarly, in the animated film, Sebastian was confused about being a lobster but in reality, it was a red Jamaican Crab.

Similarly, the fans are super excited to see the role of a famous actress played as Ariel’s six princess mermaid sisters.

  • Actress Lorena Andrea plays the role of Perla in The Little Mermaid.
  • The renowned Bridgerton actress Simone Ashley portrays the character of Indira.
  • Kajsa Mohammar is seen as Ariel eldest sister Karina in The Little Mermaid.
  • Nathalie Sorell played the role of Caspia, the remake version of Adella.
  • Karolina Conchet played the role of Mala in the famous remake of The Little Mermaid.
  • Sienna King is seen in Live-action The Little Mermaid as Tamika.
