Congratulations! X Men director Bryan Singer and his best friend Michelle Clunie have welcomed a baby boy into the world.

The delighted dad made the announcement with a photograph of the happy trio taken moments after his birth, with Singer still in scrubs while Queer as Folk actress Clunie cradles her newborn son.

"Michelle Clunie and I would like to welcome our beautiful son Dashiell Julius William Clunie-Singer into the world :)," the proud dad captioned the intimate family snap which he shared via Twitter in the late hours of Monday, Jan. 5.

Singer, 49, and Clunie, 44, have been close pals for the past 25 years, and released a statement to the Hollywood Reporter back in October announcing their happy baby news.
